Syllabus
======
* **Goal:** Equip students with the necessary basic, so that you can apply this to more complex experiments and learn more advanced analysis techniques
* Introduction to EEG & ERP analysis for students with little or no prior EEG experience
* Basic understanding of what can be assessed via EEG, how to interpret ERP results and how to preprocess / analyze EEG / ERP data
* Practical knowledge and hands-on experience on how to record clean EEG data
* Blended learning with on-site meetings, online course material, and Zoom consultation hours

Course Overview
======
* Block 1: Pre-Meeting
    * Introduction / General course overview and course objectives
* Block 2: Intro to EEG basics & first steps in MATLAB
    * Introduction to EEG basics
    * Introduction to the paradigm
    * Laboratory preparation / how to measure EEG
    * MATLAB Intro Video
* Block 3: On-site meeting with data collection
    * Hands-on sessions in which students learn to conduct an EEG study. Students learn to 
        * prepare the EEG cap and the subject
        * conduct an EEG experiment and to measure physiological signals
        * clean the cap and the working environment
* Block 4: Preprocessing - Intro
    * EEG channels
    * Data Cleaning: Filtering
    * Data Cleaning: Channel Rejection
    * From continuous EEG data to epochs
    * Independent Component Analysis (ICA)
    * ICA as a tool for artifact rejection
* Block 5: Preprocessing & EEG Data Analysis
    * Preprocessing of EEG data: MATLAB scripts & Questions
    * From preprocessed data to analysis (STUDY design & how to quantify ERP components)
* Block 6: Your turn!
    * Detailed information on the requirements will follow.
* Block 7: Prepare your questions
    * Questions
    * Feedback & Evaluation
    * Outlook: What else is possible with EEG?
